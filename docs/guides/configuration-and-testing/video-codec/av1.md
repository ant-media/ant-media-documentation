# AV1

AV1 is a next-generation, open-source video codec designed to deliver significantly better compression efficiency than H264 and H265. It enables high-quality video at lower bitrates, making it ideal for bandwidth-optimized streaming.

---

## Why Use AV1?

- **Highest Compression Efficiency:** Better quality at lower bitrates compared to H264 and H265  
- **Future-Ready Codec:** Designed for modern streaming platforms and next-gen devices  
- **Bandwidth Savings:** Ideal for large-scale streaming deployments. 

---

## Limitations

- **Limited Browser Support:** Not all browsers fully support AV1 in WebRTC. You can review this page to know more- https://caniuse.com/?search=AV1
- **High CPU Usage:** Encoding and decoding AV1 requires significant processing power   

---

## Enable AV1 Codec

![AV1](/img/configuration-and-testing/AV1.webp)

From version 3.0.1 onward, AV1 can be enabled directly from the Application Settings.

- Navigate to **Application Settings**
- Enable **AV1 codec**
- Save the configuration
